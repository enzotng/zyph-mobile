Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

ZYPH is a group-travel mobile app (planning, expense sharing, travel assistant). This policy explains what personal data we process, why, and the rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data controller

The data controller is Enzo Tang, publisher of the ZYPH app. For any question about your data: enzotang@live.fr.

2. Data we process

Account and identity

  • Email address, and optionally the display name and profile photo you provide.
  • If you use "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Apple": the provider identifier and the email shared. With Apple's "Hide My Email", we only receive a relay address.
  • Your password is managed and encrypted by our authentication provider; we cannot access it.

Trip content

  • Trips, itinerary events, expenses and their receipts, balances, recorded settlements, packing lists, members and documents you add.
  • Photos you upload (avatar, trip cover, expense receipts).

Location

  • Your precise location is processed only if you enable location sharing within a trip, to show members on the map. You can turn it off at any time.

Device and notifications

  • A push notification token (device identifier) if you allow notifications.

AI assistant (Zo) and smart import

  • When you use the assistant, expense import or packing-list generation, a text summary of the trip and your request are sent to our AI provider to produce the response. Do not enter unnecessary sensitive information.

We use no third-party advertising or behavioural analytics trackers.

5. Transfers outside the EU

Our application data is hosted in the United Kingdom, which benefits from a European Commission adequacy decision. Some providers (notably AI and push) are located in the United States. These transfers are covered by appropriate GDPR safeguards (standard contractual clauses and/or adequacy mechanisms).

6. Retention

Your data is kept while your account is active. When you delete your account from the app: your personal trips are deleted and your profile is anonymised. To preserve the integrity of trips shared with other travellers, shared expense history is kept in an anonymised form and account access is disabled.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and portability, as well as the right to withdraw consent.

  • You can delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete account).
  • To exercise your other rights: enzotang@live.fr.
  • You may lodge a complaint with the French authority CNIL (www.cnil.fr).

8. Security

Traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Data access is isolated per user at the database level, and the local session is stored encrypted on your device.

9. Children

ZYPH is not directed at people under 15 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Changes

This policy may change. The last-updated date above reflects the version in force.
